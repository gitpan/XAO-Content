<%Page path="/hints/bits/header"%> <%Styler text="Content -- Name Syntax" style="hint-title"%> Content name uniquely refers to a dynamic content element throughout the site.

Allowed characters are letters of english alphabet, digits and underscore sign. Maximum length is limited to 30 characters.

Usually names are grouped by areas with are name first, then underscore and some name inside of that group.

Examples: 

  about_management
  about_staff
  contact_ca
  contact_nj
